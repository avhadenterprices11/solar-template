🔹 3. PROJECT OVERVIEW
The Smart Metering Installation Project focused on modernizing existing energy metering systems by replacing conventional meters with advanced smart meters across multiple locations.
G&D Brothers executed large-scale, distributed installations at individual consumer premises, ensuring accurate metering, system integration, and consistent deployment across varied site conditions.

🔹 4. SCOPE OF WORK
Replacement of 5000+ existing meters with smart meters
Site survey and identification of existing installations
Safe removal and handling of old meters
Installation of smart meters including mounting, wiring, and configuration
Electrical integration with existing consumer supply systems
Phase alignment and load connection verification
Testing and validation of installed meters
Documentation and record maintenance for each installation
Coordination with stakeholders for access and scheduling
Ensuring compliance with safety and utility requirements
