# Implementation Plan: G&D Brothers "About Us" Page

This document outlines the phase-wise implementation of the **About Us** page for G&D Brothers, designed to showcase corporate legacy, leadership, and operational scale.

---

## 🎨 Design Ethos
- **Style**: "Industrial Corporate Portfolio" — High-trust, professional, and engineering-focused.
- **Micro-Interactions**: Parallax scrolls, staggered text reveals, and high-quality photography overlays.
- **Trust Elements**: Highlighted credentials (B.E. Electrical, Chartered Engineer) and government affiliations.
